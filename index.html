About Me

Things about me

Hello! My name is Milan Lizriadi. I am an Indonesian web developer and programmer. I have been programming since I was 12 years old, and I have a passion for creating websites and applications that are not only functional but also visually appealing. I am always eager to learn new technologies and improve my skills. I believe that programming is not just about writing code, but also about solving problems and creating solutions that can make a difference in people's lives. I am excited to share my journey with you through this portfolio website, where you can find some of my projects and learn more about my work.

Halo! Nama saya Milan Lizriadi. Saya seorang pengembang web dan programmer Indonesia.
